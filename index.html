Personal stylist Tatiana Sullivan

As personal stylist I will help you update your wardrobe.

I can provide advice on what to wear and when, as well as offer suggestions for how to mix-and-match your clothes.

Some people find that they save money by only purchasing the items that their stylist recommends, because they are getting higher quality pieces without having to shop around.

A personal stylist is someone who helps you dress better.

I can help with everything from what to wear for your job interview, to how to coordinate an outfit for a night out on the town.

I offer my expertise in fashion and style, as well as an objective opinion about what will look best on you.
